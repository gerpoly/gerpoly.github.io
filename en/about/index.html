Brief Introduction of the Gerpoly Plastic GmbH

  Gerpoly Plastic GmbH is a German company, which is specialized in producing and selling high-grade agricultural film. Production equipment for 5-layer co-extrusion film uses the German technology, which can produce 1-19 meters wide width of high-grade PO film.

  The company mainly produces HEPP Film, Light Diffusion Long Life Time Film, Light Diffusion Long Life Time & Anti-Dripping Film, and other film series.

  The company always insisted on purchasing the chemical material from international well-known companies, such as ExxonMobil, BASF, Dow, etc., to provide the best films for customers. It has world-class 5-layer co-extrusion ultra-wide blown film equipment and automatic on-line thickness measuring equipment. All the advantages of Materials and Equipments improve the company competitiveness.

  The company is located in Dusseldorf. Dusseldorf is close to the world-famous Ruhr Industrial Zone and is the capital of North Rhine-Westphalia. Düsseldorf is an important city for advertising, clothing, exhibition and communications in Germany and it is the European logistics center city. Good location advantages and industrial base make it began to attract foreign investors to settle here long time ago. Many famous German companies’ headquarters are located here, such as MannesMann, VEBA, and Henkel. The world well-known companies in the field of global chemistry and Life Science, such as Bayer, Johnson, 3M medical and etc. have established the office in Dusseldorf and its surrounding areas.

  There are also many Chinese famous companies here. HUAWEI took a fancy to the location advantage of Dusseldorf, and moved its Western Europe headquarters from Britain to Düsseldorf. ZTE’s headquarters are located here. Hisense group established the European headquarters in Dusseldorf.

  Nordrhein-Westfalen is located in western Germany, it is the most populated and economically developed region in Europe, and north of Nordrhein-Westfalen is the center of agriculture and animal husbandry.

Why Choose US？

5-layer co-extrusion ultra-wide blown film equipment

Five-layer co-extrusion Equipment uses German Technology. Compare with three-layer co-extrusion, Five-layer co-extrusion has more functional layers, and additives can be added more flexibly.

automatic on-line thickness measuring equipment

Using automatic on-line thickness measuring equipment can control and adjust the thickness automatically, so the thickness deviation is very small, the actual life time is longer than the warranty life time.

The Advantage of Five-layer Co-extrusion

Five-layer co-extrusion process can make each kind of functional additive added in each layer, so each additive can work better. Five-layer co-extrusion has more functional layers, and additives can be added more flexibly.
