Agriculture and the Future Development

  German agriculture is well developed, and the mechanization is very high. The agricultural in Germany is mainly small and medium-sized family farms. The main agricultural products in Germany are milk, pork, beef, grain and sugar beet, and some areas are mainly based on fruits and vegetables. It export more efficient agricultural products and import some labor-intensive agricultural products.

  As a strong European country, the development of German agriculture is also the trend of world agricultural development.

The trend of agricultural development in the future:

  1. 1) Highly modernized
  2. 2) Functional diversification.
      Agriculture has not only the industry to provide food and feed, but also has become the renewable raw materials supply department, to provide a lot of bio-energy, as well as it is the place of the natural environment protection and tourism.
  3. 3) High-quality Product
      All food must meet strict food regulations, and establish the efficient food quality traceability system. Consumers can choose different quality grades, product identification, enterprise quality marks, national green logo, etc. to ensure food safety.
  4. 4) High efficiency Production
  5. 5) Service specialization
      There are many organizations service the agricultural, including all kinds of rural cooperatives, agricultural research institutions, etc., can supply specialized services for agricultural enterprises, such as science and technology, information, machinery, etc..
  6. 6) Eco-agriculture products are favored customers because of its environmental safety.
