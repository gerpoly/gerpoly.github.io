HEPP Film

HEPP Film is a kind of PO film. The lifetime of anti-dripping function is as long as the PO film itself. With Five-layer co-extrusion processing technology, EVA content is up to 80%, and it has very excellent light transmission and insulation properties.

Light Diffusion Long Life Time & Anti-Dripping Film

Light diffusion long life time & anti-dripping film is mainly aimed at the crops at strong sunlight area and needs certain anti-dripping function, in ensuring the premise of light transmission, to make the light diffuse to avoid burning crops.

Light Diffusion Long Life Time Film

Light diffusion long life time film is developed for strong sunlight area, according to the different crops on the demand of the light to develop different diffuse degree of the product. The film has long-term and anti-aging function.

Black-White Film

Black-White Film is used the multi-layer co-extrusion process, and added the imported additives. As one side is white and the other is black, when use the film, the white side can reflect the sunlight, and the black side can block the light, so as to stop the light completely.
