HEPP Film

HEPP is a kind of PO film. Coating the anti-dripping agent and anti-fogging agent on the film surface and drying, to make lifetime of anti-dripping function is as long as the PO film itself. With Five-layer co-extrusion processing technology, EVA content is up to 80%, and it has very excellent light transmission and insulation properties. It uses BASF anti-aging agent, the longest service life is more than 5 years.

(1) Anti-dripping Effect

Long life time & anti-dripping film, the anti-dripping agent is added in the inner of the film, and the water vapor is condensed on the inner surface of the film to form water droplets, sunlight is refracted so as to reduce the transmittance of the film, and about after 4 months the anti-dripping agent gradually separate out, and the film will no longer have the anti-dripping effect, and drip will cause disease. Compared with long life time & anti-dripping film, PO Film because of its surface coating, the water vapor is condensed and form a layer of water film on the surface of the film, it not only does not affect the light through, but also not drip, the anti-dripping and anti-fogging period can reach 5 years.

(3) Aging Property

Pesticide contain volatile S and Cl, these two elements into the film will react with the adding anti-aging agent. HEPP film using the Germany BASF anti-ageing agent, it has very good anti-pesticide, the normal product can be resistant to S Content of 1200ppm, Cl content of 600~800ppm, high anti-pesticide can be resistant to S content of 3000ppm.
