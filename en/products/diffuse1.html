Light Diffusion Long Life Time Film

   Light diffusion long life time film is developed for strong sunlight area, according to the different crops on the demand of the light to develop different diffuse degree of the product. We use BASF anti-aging agent, with add a certain amount of ultraviolet absorbent, to make the film has long-term anti-aging function.

   We can see the sky is bright, that because the sun is scattered by the atmosphere before reaching the ground. It has no atmosphere on the moon, so except the direct light source, the rest are black. Diffuse light can uniformly reach to the leaf of crop and give the more light to crops. The increasing of light transmittance proportion can lower peak temperature at noon in order to protect leaves from burning on the precondition of same light transmittance. In the meantime, diffuse light can even reach the root of crops which facilitate the photosynthesis of lower-middle part of crops, and boost the utilization of light. Greenhouse cultivation in Holland shows that diffuse light can improve yield of crops and make crops appear in the market ahead of time.
